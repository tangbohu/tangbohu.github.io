Biography

Keke Tang is an associate professor at Guangzhou University since January 2019. From 2017.8 to 2018.12, he worked as a postdoctoral fellow in Prof. Wenping Wang's group (IEEE Fellow) of the University of Hong Kong.

Keke Tang received his B.S. from Jilin University in 2012, and Ph.D. from University of Science and Technology of China supervised by Prof. Xiaoping Chen and Dr. Peng Song, in 2017.
