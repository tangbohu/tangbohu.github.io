Biography

Keke Tang is a Full Professor at Guangzhou University since July 2025. He served as an Associate Professor from January 2019 to June 2025. From August 2017 to December 2018, he worked as a postdoctoral fellow in Prof. Wenping Wang's group (IEEE & ACM Fellow) at the University of Hong Kong.

Keke Tang received his B.S. from Jilin University in 2012, and Ph.D. from University of Science and Technology of China supervised by Prof. Xiaoping Chen in 2017, where he also closely worked with Prof. Peng Song and Prof. Ligang Liu.
